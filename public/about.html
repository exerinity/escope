About escope

escope is a Cloudflare Worker, made with TypeScript. Scopes can last from 1 minute to 3 days (4320 minutes) but can be retracted early, if you made it. Upon the expiry time of the scope, it becomes inaccessible and annulled.

Scopes are tied to your IP to prevent abuse and to enable management of your own scopes without any accounts.

Scopes are handled as KV entries and immediately processed with a fast 302 redirect.

The premise is simple: enter a link, and how long that link should work, and optionally, a slug mode. Then, share that link anywhere! But, ideally not everywhere... because, you know, it expires.

When could escope be useful? Well, say, you just minted a fresh Virtual Machine, and you want to get the exact file links for certain software, but clipboard access to your host doesn't work! Make a very short-lived scope for it, and type it in on the VM! Or, another physical computer!
