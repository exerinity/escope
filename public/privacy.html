Privacy

TL;DR

  • escope dies not store accounts, use cookies analytics, or ads
  • The URL you submit and your IP address is stored. Nothing else
  • Your IP is used to show you your own scopes and to enforce rate limits
  • New scopes are logged for moderation, including the target URL and your IP

What is collected

  • The target URL you submit
  • A randomly generated slug
  • The time a scope was created and when it expires
  • Your IP address

What it's used for

  • Running the redirect until the scope expires
  • Showing you the scopes you created, matched by IP
  • Letting you delete your own scopes early, matched by IP
  • Rate limiting and blocking abusive IPs

Where it goes

  • Everything is stored in Cloudflare Workers KV
  • Every new scope (target URL, resulting link, your IP, expiration) is sent to a private webhook I use to watch for abuse
  • Cloudflare processes request metadata (e.g. your IP) to serve traffic, under their own policies

Retention

  • A scope and its data are deleted the moment it expires or you destroy it
  • A per-IP list of your active slugs exists only while you have active scopes
  • If your IP gets temporarily or permanently blocked for abuse, that block record is kept indefinitely, even after your scopes are gone

Security

I collect the minimum needed to run the service and rely on Cloudflare's infrastructure for the rest. Just... don't put sensitive data behind a scope.

Your options

  • Delete a scope early with the "Destroy" button
  • Contact: exerinity.com/email
